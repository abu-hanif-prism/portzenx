About

A photographer who
listens before she looks.

"Photography isn't about what you see — it's about what you feel standing in a place no one else noticed."

I'm Mara Elen — a documentary and portrait photographer based between Copenhagen and Dhaka. I've spent 14 years searching for the image that exists just before or just after the obvious one.

My work has been published in The Guardian, Foam Magazine, and exhibited across Scandinavia and South Asia. I shoot on medium format film and digital, and develop all my own black-and-white prints.

Every engagement starts with a conversation. I don't pick up the camera until I know what we're trying to say together.
