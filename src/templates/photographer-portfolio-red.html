I believe a photograph should make you feel something before you understand it. My work sits at the intersection of documentary honesty and fine-art intention — images that are both true and considered.

"The best photograph is the one that captures an emotion you didn't know you were feeling until you saw it."

Based between Berlin and Dhaka, I shoot portraits, landscapes, editorial campaigns, and long-form documentary projects. My work has appeared in publications across Europe and South Asia.

Every image in this portfolio was shot on 35mm or medium format film — no digital manipulation beyond basic exposure correction.
