About Dr. Mitchell

Medicine rooted in
compassion & science

"Every patient deserves a physician who listens first, and acts with both precision and humanity."

Dr. Sarah Mitchell is a board-certified cardiologist and Senior Attending Physician at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital. With over 18 years of clinical experience, she specialises in interventional cardiology and heart failure.

She completed her MD at Harvard Medical School, residency at Johns Hopkins, and a cardiology fellowship at the Cleveland Clinic. She is a Fellow of the American College of Cardiology and has been named a Top Doctor by New York Magazine five consecutive years.
